## Production Notes

**Screen Recording:**
- Use OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- 1920x1080 resolution
- 60fps for smooth motion
- Use cursor highlighting for clicks

**Voiceover:**
- Clear, confident delivery
- Slightly faster pacing (compress 70s script to 60s)
- Add subtle background music (royalty-free from Epidemic Sound)

**Editing:**
- Use DaVinci Resolve (free) or iMovie
- Add text overlays with motion graphics
- Subtle zoom/pan on static screens
- Include captions for accessibility

**Export Settings:**
- MP4, H.264 codec
- 1920x1080, 30fps
- Under 50MB for easy upload

**Upload To:**
- Product Hunt (main demo)
- Twitter (launch tweet)
- YouTube (embed on homepage)
- Loom (quick share link)
